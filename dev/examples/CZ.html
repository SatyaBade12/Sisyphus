CZ gate in Rydberg atoms

\[H = \frac{\Omega(t)}{2}\left[e^{i\phi(t)} (\sigma_+^1 + \sigma_+^2) + e^{-i\phi(t)} (\sigma_-^1+\sigma_-^2)\right] - \Delta(t) (n_1 + n_2) + V n_1n_2\]
